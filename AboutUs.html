ABOUT KWAMZ


Kwamz is a firm that was started by Mr Takudzwa Prosper Kwambana. He started the firm in January 2021. The firm repairs Apple products. It focuses on importing Apple gadgets parts into Zimbabwe and offers repairal services to the local people.


Vision


Our vision is to make sure that the company grows and starts to offer its services to all parts of Zimbabwe even to those in the rural areas.
